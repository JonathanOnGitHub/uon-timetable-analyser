University of Nottingham Timetable Staff & Module Load Analyser

Note: All processing happens locally in your browser, no data leaves your computer. To use this app, simpply upload your HTML timetable files (download from the timetabling website) to analyse staff teaching loads. Multiple files are supported, which may be useful if your school runs multiple courses and you wish o calculate total staff loads. Tip - you likely want to activate the "avoid double-counting" setting.
